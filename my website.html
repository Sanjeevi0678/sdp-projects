About chennai:

  • Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of Tamil Nadu, located on the Coromandel Coast along the Bay of Bengal.
  • The city has a rich history dating back over 400 years. It was founded in 1639 when the British East India Company built Fort St. George, which later became the center of trade and administration.
  • Chennai is famous for its Marina Beach, one of the longest urban beaches in the world.
  • The city is a cultural capital, known for Carnatic music, Bharatanatyam dance, and Tamil cinema (Kollywood). It is also an important center for information technology, automobile manufacturing, and healthcare.
  • Today, Chennai is a vibrant blend of history, tradition, and modern development, attracting visitors from around the world.
